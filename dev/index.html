Disclaimer:

- Works best on a desktop. Use rotated sideways (Landscape) for Mobile.
- The right ramps and the decking itself will look better in game. Hopefully clean up the proper edges in future versions.
- Supercell designed the shapes, locations, etc. This tool just shows what is possible.
- I am bad at HTML/Javascript/CSS, be gentle!
- Supercell HAS shown interest in community submitted maps but it's unknown how often or how fast they will implement them.

Guidelines:

- There must be a path for troops to exit the ramp where they are dropped.
- All troops (ground and air) must be able to attack all engine rooms.
- A deck that is used at the 2 ER level must be available for the entire season. You cannot remove it as buildings would be displaced.
- There has to be sufficient room for all defenses.
- Ramps 1 & 2, 2 & 3 or 1 & 2 & 3 can be used at the same time. 1 & 3 is not a valid configuration (feature requested for future seasons).

Sharing:

- After building a deck please upload your screenshots to an album on Imgur and then share on the Boom Beach Discord, Telegram, Reddit, etc.
- Make sure your screenshot includes the deck/ramp numbers for ease of re-creation
- Select the appropriate ER level for each screenshot!
